Legal AI in India — the common questions

What is the best AI legal tool for lawyers in India?
Headnote is an AI legal assistant built specifically for Indian advocates. Unlike general chatbots or foreign legal-AI tools, it drafts court-ready applications in Hindi and English, researches Indian case law with citations verified against their source, and is built on the new BNS, BNSS and BSA codes. It serves every court in India from the Supreme Court to the district bench.
Can AI draft legal documents in Hindi?
Yes. Headnote drafts court-ready litigation documents in Hindi and English — bail applications, discharge applications, maintenance petitions, complaints and civil suits — in the format the court expects. It also supports voice-first drafting, so you can dictate in Hindi.
Does Headnote invent or hallucinate case law?
No. Zero fabrication is a core principle. Every citation is checked against its source before it appears in a draft or research result; if a case cannot be verified it is flagged rather than presented as fact. Headnote never invents client facts, dates or judgments.
