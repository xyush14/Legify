how it works

three modes. that's it.

not a chatbot. a research instrument with three specific operations, each with its own output discipline.

01/

situation → cases

describe your matter in plain english. get five precedents matched to your facts, each with a verified citation and a one-line note.

output → ranked precedents
02/

topic → digest

type a doctrinal topic. get a research digest organised under sub-topic headings with leading authority and quotable phrases.

output → research digest
03/

judgment → headnote

paste any criminal judgment. get journal-format lettered headnotes — one per discrete point of law — and a cases-referred list.

output → cri.l.j. format
