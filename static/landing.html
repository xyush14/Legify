Straight answers

No surprises. Everything upfront.

Is the demo really free?

Yes. Three days, no card. You get real research queries, drafts, Section Finder lookups and unlimited judgment reads. Your saved work stays when the demo ends; pay only if you'd actually file what Headnote drafts.

Will it ever invent a citation?

No. Every citation is checked against the source and shown only when it resolves; anything unverified is flagged, never fabricated. Zero invented citations is the hard line.

What can Headnote draft?

Any application, in your court's own format, Hindi or English. Bail, discharge, revision, appeal, maintenance, replies and beyond. It is not a fixed list of templates. Describe the matter and Headnote drafts it.

Will I be auto-charged?

Never. Monthly and Yearly are one-time payments that unlock 30 or 365 days of access. Nothing renews on its own; you come back and pay only when you need it.

How do I pay, and what about refunds?

UPI, cards, netbanking or wallets via an RBI-licensed gateway. Prices include GST and an invoice is available on request. See our Refund Policy for details.
