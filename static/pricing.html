? Straight answers

No surprises. Everything upfront.

01Is the demo really free?

Yes — 3 days, no card. You get 3 research queries, 2 drafts, 5 Section Finder lookups and unlimited judgment reads. When the demo ends your saved work stays put; upgrade only if you'd actually file what Headnote drafts.

02Does "unlimited" have a catch?

No. On Monthly and Yearly, research, drafts, judgment reads and the Section Finder run without caps. The number you see is the number that's enforced in the backend — there are no fair-use asterisks or throttles hidden in the fine print.

03What's the ₹99 Section Finder?

A one-time, lifetime unlock of the IPC↔BNS, CrPC↔BNSS and Evidence↔BSA finder — what changed, the punishment grid, classification and a plain-English summary. Everyone gets 5 lookups free; it's included at no extra cost in Monthly and Yearly.

04Will I be auto-charged?

Never. Monthly and Yearly are one-time payments that unlock 30 or 365 days of access. Nothing renews on its own — you come back and buy again only when you need it.

05How do I pay, and what about refunds?

UPI, cards, netbanking or wallets via Cashfree (RBI-licensed). Prices include GST and an invoice is available on request. See our Refund Policy for details.
